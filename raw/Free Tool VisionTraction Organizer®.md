### Free Tool

Get everyone in your company rowing in the same direction by getting a clear picture of where your company is going and how you’re going to get there.

Strategic planning is often too complex, and an organization’s vision is seldom clear and concise. The Vision/Traction Organizer (V/TO) is a powerful tool that uses eight guiding questions to help you:

- Simplify the strategic planning process
- Distill your vision into simple, understandable points
- Map out where you’re going and how you’re going to get there
- Get your vision out of your head and onto paper
