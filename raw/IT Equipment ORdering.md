# IT Equipment Ordering SOP

**Owner:** IT

**Author:** Alexander Thiel

**Overview:** This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) guides the ordering of computing and technology equipment to ensure all technology is appropriate, provides value for money, and integrates with existing Clesen technology where applicable. The objective is to minimize hardware diversity within the company for easier maintenance and cost control.

## Equipment Configuration Policy

Standardization of Clesen technology resources is essential due to variances in types, makes, models, configurations, builds, versions, and brands, which simplifies service, maintenance, and cost management.

All employees must request and use equipment recommended by the Clesen IT Function. A comprehensive hardware list is impractical due to changing availability, so consult IT for current specifications before any purchase.

This policy covers all IT technology, including but not limited to:  
- Computers (Desktop, Laptop, Tablets, Mobile Devices, etc.)  
- HDTVs  
- Printers, scanners, copiers, fax machines, or all-in-one devices  
- Projectors, screens, and Smart Boards  
- Digital cameras and camcorders  
- Software (Application, Operating System, Network-Based, etc.)  
- Other technology (docking stations, monitors, keyboards, etc.)

## Ordering Process

### Step 1: Obtain Manager Approval
**Responsible:** User, Manager

Discuss your equipment requirement with your manager and obtain their approval before proceeding with an equipment request.

---

### Step 2: Submit Equipment Request

**Responsible:** User

1. Navigate to [Clesen IT Support - Home](https://clesenwholesale.sharepoint.com/sites/IT/help/SitePages/IT-Equipment-Request.aspx)
2. Create a new equipment request with detailed specifications

---

### Step 3: Check Equipment Availability
**Responsible:** IT Department

IT will check inventory for available equipment:
- **If available:** Equipment will be handed out to the requester
- **If installation needed:** IT will schedule and perform installation

---

### Step 4: Determine Costs
**Responsible:** IT Manager

1. Request is routed to IT Manager
2. IT Manager determines equipment cost

---

### Step 5: IT Manager Approval
**Responsible:** IT Manager

IT Manager reviews and approves the request:
- **If cost < $100:** Proceed directly to purchase
- **If cost ≥ $100:** Route to manager approval (Step 6)

---

### Step 6: Department Manager Approval
**Responsible:** Requester's Manager

For purchases **above $100**, requester's manager approval is required. Approval request is sent automatically after IT Manager approval.

---

### Step 7: Purchase Equipment
**Responsible:** IT Department

1. Coordinate with Purchasing department
2. If equipment ships to a Clesen location, create PO in ERP system
3. Proceed with vendor order

---

### Step 8: Process Payment
**Responsible:** IT Department

Follow Accounting department guidance for payment processing.

---

### Step 9: Equipment Delivery
**Responsible:** IT Department

**Standard:** All equipment is delivered to IT department

**Exception:** Equipment too large or heavy to move internally may be delivered directly to end location

---

### Step 10: Equipment Receiving
**Responsible:** Location Manager

1. Follow Product Receiving SOP
2. Store equipment safely
3. Notify IT department of receipt

---

### Step 11: Installation and Configuration
**Responsible:** IT Department / MSP

MSP (Managed Service Provider) or IT performs:
- Equipment installation
- System configuration
- User setup and training (if applicable)
