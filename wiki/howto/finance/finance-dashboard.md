# Finance Dashboard

Real-time executive dashboard for monitoring key financial metrics including revenue, profitability, cash flow, and operational efficiency.

---

## What This Is

The Finance Dashboard provides financial performance visibility at a glance for executives and finance managers. It displays:

- **Revenue metrics** — Year-to-date sales and current period performance
- **Profitability** — Gross margin, net income, cost of goods sold
- **Cash flow** — Available cash position, accounts receivable, accounts payable
- **Operational metrics** — Efficiency ratios and performance trends

**Key benefits:**

- Real-time visibility without manual reporting
- Data-driven decision-making
- Trend analysis vs. targets
- Drill-down to underlying transactions
- Anytime access vs. waiting for monthly reports

---

## When to Use It

Use the Finance Dashboard when you need to:

- Monitor real-time financial performance
- Check key financial metrics at a glance
- Compare current performance to targets
- Drill down into specific metric details
- Present financial status to stakeholders

---

## How to Access

### Via the main menu

1. Navigate to **Dashboards** > **Finance Dashboard** or **Reports** > **Finance Dashboard**
2. Dashboard loads with current financial data

### Bookmarking for quick access

1. Open the Finance Dashboard
2. **Ctrl+D** (Windows) or **Cmd+D** (Mac) to bookmark
3. Name it "Finance Dashboard" for easy reference

### Setting as your home page

1. Open the Finance Dashboard
2. Click **⋯** (More) > **Set as Home Page** or **Default View**
3. Dashboard appears automatically on next login

---

## Dashboard Layout

### Quick Metrics Ribbon (Top)

Key figures at a glance:

| Metric | What It Measures |
|--------|------------------|
| **Year-to-Date Revenue** | Total sales received to date |
| **Year-to-Date Expenses** | Total operational expenses to date |
| **Gross Profit Margin** | (Revenue - COGS) ÷ Revenue (%) |
| **Current Period Net Income** | Profit/loss for current accounting period |
| **Cash Position** | Current available cash balance |
| **Accounts Receivable (A/R)** | Outstanding customer invoices not yet paid |

### Detailed Sections

Dashboard includes expandable sections for:

- **Revenue analysis** — By customer, by product category, by region
- **Expense breakdown** — By department, by type (COGS, Operating, Overhead)
- **Profitability trends** — Monthly/quarterly performance vs. targets
- **Cash flow statement** — Inflows, outflows, net change
- **Key ratios** — Margin %, ROI, turnover rates
- **Budget vs. actual** — Spending compared to approved budgets

---

## Interpreting Metrics

**Green indicators** = Meeting or exceeding target
**Yellow indicators** = Approaching threshold or below target
**Red indicators** = Significant deviation from target

Hover over any metric for:
- Historical comparison (previous period)
- Trend direction (up/down)
- Performance vs. budget

---

## Drill-Down Capabilities

Click any metric to drill into underlying data:

1. Click the metric value
2. Detailed transaction list appears
3. Filter by date range, department, or account
4. Export data if needed
5. Use "Back" to return to dashboard

Example: Click "Year-to-Date Revenue" to see all sales transactions with details.

---

## Customizing Your View

### Filtering data

1. Click **Filter** button on dashboard
2. Select:
   - **Date range** (select period or custom dates)
   - **Department** (view specific department metrics)
   - **Product category** (focus on specific products)
   - **Customer or region** (if applicable)
3. Dashboard updates automatically

### Adjusting metric display

1. Click **⋯** (More) > **Customize Metrics**
2. Select which metrics to show/hide
3. Drag to reorder metric ribbon
4. Save your preference

---

## Refreshing Data

### Manual refresh

1. Click **Refresh** button (⟲) at the top
2. Dashboard reloads latest data from system
3. Typically updates in seconds

### Auto-refresh settings

1. Click **⋯** (More) > **Auto-Refresh**
2. Select interval: Every 5 minutes, 15 minutes, 30 minutes, or hourly
3. Dashboard automatically updates at selected interval
4. Useful for real-time monitoring during month-end close

---

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard not loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache and refresh (Ctrl+F5)
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your access permissions (contact IT if issue persists)

### Metrics showing incorrect data

1. Verify the date range filter (check the current filter settings)
2. Click **Refresh** to reload latest data
3. If still incorrect, contact Finance team to verify source data

### Drill-down not working

1. Ensure you have permission to view detailed transactions
2. Try clicking from a different metric
3. Check browser's pop-up settings (drill-down may open in pop-up)

## FAQ

**Q: How often is the data updated?**
A: Data updates near real-time from the Business Central system. Manual refresh reloads latest data immediately. Auto-refresh intervals are configurable.

**Q: Can I export the dashboard data?**
A: Yes. Click **Export** button to download as Excel, PDF, or other formats. Drill-down views also support export.

**Q: Who can see the Finance Dashboard?**
A: Users with Finance Manager or Executive role. Contact your IT administrator to request access.

**Q: Can I share the dashboard with someone outside the company?**
A: Dashboards are internal only for security reasons. Generate a PDF report to share externally.

**Q: What if a metric looks wrong?**
A: First, verify the date range and filters. Then click drill-down to inspect underlying transactions. If data is still incorrect, notify your Finance manager.
